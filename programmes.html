Programmes

Viksit Bharat Ambassador - Yuva Connect Programme

The idea is to take the PM’s vision of Viksit Bharat to maximum youth by conducting 50 programs in educational institutions in various states across the country.

Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue

A national youth festival in 2025 that will bring together young leaders from a variety of backgrounds, including the Viksit Bharat Challenge, district and state-level youth festivals, and entrepreneurship, sports, agriculture, and technology

India's Techade: Chips for Viksit Bharat

A program to strengthen the semiconductor ecosystem in India, including the establishment of semiconductor fabs in Morigaon, Assam and Sanand

Sashakt Nari – Viksit Bharat

A program that includes schemes to address issues related to women, such as Beti Bachao Beti Padhao, Sukanya Samriddhi, Mudra Yojana, maternity leave expansion, free medical treatment, and affordable medicines
